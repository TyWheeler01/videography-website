Frequently asked questions

Do you Travel?

Yes! I'd love to capture your big day no matter where you are located. I am based in the Massachusetts in the Bristol County region. Travel is often included in packages however, if your wedding is considered long-distance travel charges may be applicable. If flight and or hotel stay is required, these charges may be applied additionally. Reach out and I'll get a quote to you that includes travel costs with your package selection.

How are packages structured?

I believe that each couple has a unique vision for their wedding video, and I strive to provide packages that accommodate their individual needs and preferences.

Current package offerings can be provided through a pricing brochure when contacting me. The starting price for the lowest tier package offering is currently $1,900+.

When selecting a package, I always recommend that couples consider the level of coverage they need, the length and style of the final video, and any specific requests or special moments they want captured. As a professional, I am available to discuss these options and help couples select a package that fits their budget and meets their expectations.

My ultimate goal is to provide couples with a wedding video that they will cherish for a lifetime. I understand that this is a significant investment, and I want to ensure that couples are completely satisfied with their package and the final product.

How long until we receive the final film?

Final short film delivery takes between 2 and 4 weeks.

How do we receive our film?

The final film will be delivered through a link on "VidFlow". This is essentially a little personal website dedicated to your wedding film. You can watch, download and stream your love story all through the link. If your package included a physical USB then it will be shipped immediately upon completion of the film.

What is the booking process?

  1. Initial contact
    Via email or DM
  2. Meet
    In person or video call
  3. Read & sign contract
    Contract will be sent via email

    Pay using bank details provided in email
  6. Q & A
    Complete questionnaire via email
  7. Wedding day
    It's finally here!
  8. Final delivery
    Your love story on the big screen!
